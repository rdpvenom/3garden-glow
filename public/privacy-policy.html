Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-19

GardenGlow (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. By using GardenGlow, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect non-personal information automatically, such as browser type, device type, operating system, pages visited, session duration, referral source, and approximate location based on IP address. This data helps us understand how visitors use our content and improve the website.

We may also collect personal information you voluntarily provide, such as your name and email address, for example when you contact us or subscribe to updates. We only collect personal data you choose to submit.

2. How We Use Information

We may use data to:

  • Operate and improve website functionality and content.
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • Analyze audience behavior to improve editorial strategy.
  • Prevent spam, abuse, and unauthorized activity.
  • Display advertising and evaluate ad performance.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies to remember preferences and analyze traffic. Some cookies are essential, while others support analytics and advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some site features may not work properly.

4. Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

We may display ads through Google AdSense and other ad partners. These vendors may use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve personalized ads based on your browsing activity.

You can manage ad personalization through Google Ads Settings and opt out via www.aboutads.info.

5. Analytics Services

We may use analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics) to gather aggregate data such as page views, session duration, bounce rate, and referral sources. This helps us improve content quality and navigation.

6. Third-Party Links

Our content may link to external websites. We are not responsible for third-party content or policies. Please review their privacy practices before providing personal information.

7. Data Security

We apply reasonable safeguards to protect information against unauthorized access and misuse. However, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for legitimate legal, operational, and business purposes. Aggregate non-personal data may be retained longer for reporting and analysis.

9. Children’s Privacy

GardenGlow is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Contact us below to make a request.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted with a revised date. Continued use indicates acceptance of updates.
