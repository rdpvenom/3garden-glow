Seasonal Planting Made Easy

Gardening success depends on timing. In spring, focus on soil nutrition and seed starts. In summer, protect roots with mulch and water deeply in the morning. In autumn, enrich your beds with compost and plan cool-season crops. In winter, prune carefully and prepare tools, pots, and planting calendars for the new year.
